#3 What's your path on becoming a web-developer?

#javascript #html #css



As I mentioned on my homepage (by the way, a lot of people always mix the two terms 'homepage' and 'webpage') before, it is really not easy to start to code. Why is this so? I would say there are two main reasons that reinforce this fact.


#1: education is way behind industry


I remember the first contact with computers at my school. The equipment was already oldschool back in the 90ies! But this was just hardware. You can 'update' your equipment by spending money (ok, that's easier said than done in the public sector!). But the real problem is knowledge. You have to keep up with the industry regarding standards and technology. This fact getting worse because as a teacher you have to be on top to tell the people what to learn.
